Lye face down on the floor or an exercise mat. Fully extend your arms in front of you. This is the starting position. Now simultaneously raise your arms legs and chest off the floor and hold the contraction for two seconds. In the fully contracted position, you should look like superman when he's flying. Slowly lower your arms legs and chest back down to the starting position. Slowly lower your arms legs. Slowly lower your arms. Slowly lower your arms.
