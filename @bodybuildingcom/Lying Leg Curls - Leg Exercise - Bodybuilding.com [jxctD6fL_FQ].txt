I just the machine to fit your height and life face down. The pad of the lever should be on the back of your legs a couple inches under your calves. Keep your torso flat on the bench and ensure your legs are fully stretched. Now grab the side handles of the machine and position your toes straight. This will be your starting position. Exhale and curl your legs up as far as possible without lifting your upper legs from the pad. Once you reach the fully contracted position hold for a brief moment then inhale and bring the legs back to the starting position.
